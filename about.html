How’s it going?

When the iPod Touch was released in 2007 I saved up for months to buy it. In 2008 the iOS 2.0 update was released, causing me to fall in love with the largest feature, the “App Store”. This new marketplace felt like the wild west of mobile applications, with new applications constantly being released. Ever since I have been fascinated in the interfaces behind these designs.

I graduated March of 2019 with a major in Political Science. Specializing in research during my studies, I decided to join Lambda School’s UX program to apply my experience with my passion for design. Being able to ideate and build the websites or applications I have used since my youth has been a thrilling experience. After 18 weeks of core curriculum practicing user research, ideation, usability, wireframes, high-fidelity design, Agile methodologies, HTML and CSS, I am working on an 8 week capstone, implementing and testing new features for a company.
